# Bond Scenarios 
## Portfolio 1 
### constraints 
#### target bond amount (face_value): 
- $50 million <= face_value <= $50.5 million 
#### budget (market_price*face_value): 
- directly related to target bond amount 
#### liquidity score (liquidity_score): 
- liquidity_score >= 0.85
#### time horizon (maturity_years): 
- maturity_years < 20 years 
#### expected return (expected_annual_return_pct): 
- expected_annual_return_pct > 3% (above target federal rate of inflation)
#### Bond rating (rating_bucket): 
- AAA >= 60%
- BBB + BB <= 10% 
#### Volatility (annual_volatility_pct)
- < 5%
